# Social Media Do's and Don'ts
## For College Students
### Piyush Pillay | 25BAI11482

---

## ✅ DO's

1. **Do** keep your profiles professional — use a proper name and photo.
2. **Do** think before you post — once online, content is hard to remove.
3. **Do** use privacy settings to control who sees your content.
4. **Do** give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas.
5. **Do** use social media to network professionally on platforms like LinkedIn.
6. **Do** report any cyberbullying, harassment or suspicious accounts.
7. **Do** log out of accounts on shared or public devices.

---

## ❌ DON'Ts

1. **Don't** share personal information like your address, phone number or ID online.
2. **Don't** post offensive, hateful or discriminatory content of any kind.
3. **Don't** share confidential college or exam related information online.
4. **Don't** engage with or respond to online trolls or harassers.
5. **Don't** click on suspicious links or attachments sent via social media.
6. **Don't** impersonate others or create fake accounts.
7. **Don't** post content under the influence of emotions — always pause and think.

---

*Remember: Your digital footprint is permanent. Build it wisely.*
