# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
### Piyush Pillay | 25BAI11482

---

## 🛡️ General Online Safety

1. **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP or password** with anyone — not even
   bank officials or customer care agents.
2. **Never scan a QR code** sent by an unknown person — scanning can
   debit money from your account, not credit it.
3. Always **verify the identity** of anyone contacting you about payments
   or prizes before taking any action.
4. **Do not click on suspicious links** received via SMS, WhatsApp or
   email claiming to be from your bank or UPI app.
5. **Enable two-factor authentication** on all your accounts including
   email, social media and banking apps.
6. **Use strong, unique passwords** for every account and change them
   regularly.

---

## 💰 Financial & UPI Safety (Mandatory Tips)

7. **Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money** — PIN is only required
   when sending money, never for receiving.
8. **Always double-check the recipient's UPI ID or phone number** before
   confirming any payment transaction.

---

## 📱 Device & Account Safety

9. **Do not use public Wi-Fi** for banking or UPI transactions — use
   mobile data instead.
10. **Log out of banking apps** after every session, especially on shared
    devices.

---

## 🆘 If You Are a Victim — Report Immediately

- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** cybercrime.gov.in
- **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930 (24x7, toll-free)
- **Your Bank's Fraud Helpline** — call immediately to freeze the transaction

*Report within the first hour for the best chance of recovering your money.*
